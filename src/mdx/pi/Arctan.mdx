::hr[数学逻辑]

有如下公式

```math
\begin{align}
  \arctan x &= \int \frac{1}{1+x^{2}} \mathrm{d}x \\
            &= \int \sum_{n=0}^{\infty}{(-1)^n x^{2n}} \mathrm{d}x \\
            &= \sum_{n=0}^{\infty}{(-1)^n \frac{x^{2n+1}}{2n+1}}
\end{align}
```

迭代计算即可

```math
\begin{align}
  \pi &= 4 \arctan(1) \\
      &= 4 \sum_{n=0}^{\infty}{(-1)^n \frac{1}{2n+1}} \\
      &= 4 \times (1 - \frac{1}{3} + \frac{1}{5} - \frac{1}{7} + \cdots)
\end{align}
```
